## Seasonal Training Strategy

### Summer Focus
Primary goal: Improve aerobic capacity.

Key training priorities:
- Build aerobic base
- Increase sustainable endurance duration
- Improve heart rate efficiency at sub-threshold power
- Develop fatigue resistance

Training emphasis:
- Zone 2 endurance rides
- Occasional tempo / sweet spot work
- Low to moderate intensity volume progression

### Winter Focus
Primary goal: Build muscle and strength.

Training emphasis:
- Strength training progression
- Reduced cycling volume
- Maintenance aerobic sessions
- Neuromuscular power work

## Coaching Notes

Because the athlete is a cycling beginner, the coaching approach should prioritize:

1. Aerobic base development
2. Gradual load progression
3. Avoiding excessive high intensity
4. Monitoring fatigue signals closely

Key metrics to monitor:
- Training Stress Score (TSS)
- CTL / ATL balance
- HR efficiency trends
- Aerobic decoupling
- Recovery metrics

Progression should prioritize consistency over intensity during the first training phases.
